Sweet Flypaper Film is a boutique film production company and creative studio rooted in community, independent storytelling, and the belief that the most honest films come from the most honest people.

We make features, shorts, and series. We rent gear, host productions, and develop scripts. We train new filmmakers, connect crews, and build the kind of infrastructure that lets independent artists actually make their work — without waiting for permission.

Our studio, The Shop, lives in Aurora, Colorado. Our people are everywhere.

The Sweetfly Difference

01
Community First

We exist to serve filmmakers, artists, and storytellers who don't have access to the infrastructure they deserve. We build it ourselves.

02
Honest Stories

We don't chase trends. We chase truth. The most powerful films are the ones that know exactly who they're for and aren't afraid to say so.

03
Make It Happen

We don't wait for the green light. We build the studio, write the script, find the crew, and shoot the film. That's the only way it gets done.
