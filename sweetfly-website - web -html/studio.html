The Main Stage

Whether you need a clean, infinite white backdrop for a corporate interview, or a haze-filled, dramatic lighting setup for a music video, the main floor is highly adaptable.

It’s an intimate but powerful footprint designed to give directors total control over their environment without the massive overhead of a commercial warehouse.

Flexibility

Built for Crews

We know how a set operates. The space is equipped with heavy-duty C-stands, backdrop systems, and enough room for a dedicated camera crew and talent to work comfortably.

Need extra gear? Because our rental inventory is housed right here, you can add cinema cameras, Aputure lighting, or grip gear directly to your studio booking.

Photography

Portrait & Stills

Not shooting motion? The studio is perfectly dialed in for editorial and commercial photography. Control the ambient light completely and utilize our seamless paper backdrops and strobe modifiers to get the perfect shot.

Post-Production

The Editing & Audio Suite

The work doesn't stop when the camera cuts. Our dedicated post-production suite is sound-treated and equipped for high-end editing, color grading, and audio recording.

From laying down voiceovers and podcast tracks to cutting your final feature, it's a quiet, creative sanctuary.
